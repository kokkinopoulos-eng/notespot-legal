Privacy Policy — NoteSpot Pro

Last updated: June 2026
Summary: NoteSpot Pro runs on your device. We collect no data, show no ads, and use no analytics. It additionally offers an optional Cloud AI feature that is off by default; it only works if you enable it with your own API key.

1. Data we collect

None. All your notes are stored locally on your device. We have no access to them.

2. On-device processing

Categorization, OCR, handwriting recognition, and the "Eva" assistant run locally via Google ML Kit, with no data sent anywhere.

3. Optional Cloud AI (BYOK)

NoteSpot Pro lets you connect your own AI provider API key for enhanced analysis. This feature is off by default.

If you enable it and add a key, then when you create a note, its content (text, or the note's image/audio) is sent directly from your device to the provider you selected, for analysis. NoteSpot does not proxy or store this data — communication is directly with the provider, using your key.

Supported providers and their policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude): anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Google (Gemini): ai.google.dev/gemini-api/terms
  • OpenAI: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

Use of your own key is subject to that provider's terms. Your key is stored securely on your device and is not sent anywhere other than the provider.

You can disable Cloud AI at any time; the app then reverts to fully on-device operation.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: taking photos.
  • Microphone: voice notes.
  • Notifications & Alarms: reminders.
  • Internet: downloading ML Kit models; and, if you enable Cloud AI, communicating with your chosen provider.

5. Sharing

Sharing a note happens only by your action, via Android's share system.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

7. Changes

Changes will be posted on this page.
