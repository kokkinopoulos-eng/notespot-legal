Privacy Policy — NoteSpot Free

Last updated: June 2026
Summary: NoteSpot Free runs 100% on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. No ads, no analytics.

1. What data we collect

None. All your notes (text, drawings, photos, audio, lists) are stored solely on your device. We have no access to them and they never leave your device.

2. On-device processing

Automatic categorization, text recognition in photos (OCR), handwriting recognition, and the "Eva" assistant run entirely on-device using Google ML Kit. No information is sent to any server.

3. Internet connection

The app requests internet permission only to download the on-device recognition models (ML Kit) from Google once. This download contains none of your data. Otherwise, the app does not communicate with any server.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: to take photos for notes.
  • Microphone: to record voice notes.
  • Notifications & Alarms: for note reminders.
  • Internet: only to download ML Kit models (see §3).

All permissions are used strictly on-device.

5. Sharing

When you choose to share a note, it is sent through Android's share system to the app you select. This happens only by your action.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page.
